What is Summer of Algorithms?

Summers usually come with lots of free time. Summer of Algorithms (SoA) is a place where you can pick up new skills free of cost. The program is run every year by the PyJaipur community.

The way this works is simple:

  1. First you select one of the available skill tracks. For each skill there is a sequence of tasks which need to be completed.
  2. You then read and understand the concept that the task is trying to convey.
  3. You complete the exercises for the task.

That's it!
